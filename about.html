My Story

:P

I am a studio artist/graphic desinger living in Boston MA, currently studying at Boston University. I come from a family of six, with three older siblings who are triplets. One of which is another artist living in Boston. We all grew up in a town called Andover 35 minutes north of the city. I studied at Saint Anselm College for my undergrad with a major in fine arts, and a thesis focused in sculpture and print.

Art has always been a huge part of my life, and has taken on many forms over the years. Through my childhood I had an obsession with drawing and painting. This later developed into a love for print making in college and eventually to sculpture and now graphic design. I see no lines drawn between these arts and I beleive graphic design is just as valid of fine art as painting or sculpture. CHYEEEAHHH BOIIIII
